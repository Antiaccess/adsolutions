Thanks for stopping by! I'm Anthony; a contemplative and conceptual thinker with a knack for collaboration, making sense of research & turning insights into innovative ideas.

With a background in communications & media, I’ve always been a visual storyteller driven to understand and influence my audience. I’m an iterative problem solver whose personal motto is ‘less wrong every day,’ so, naturally, I analyse and try to improve whatever I notice needs work. I am adaptable, advocate for the best solution and am resourceful in the uncertain space between research & understanding. I respect the fact that designers have influence of a subtle but powerful kind and take responsibility for shaping solutions while considering their impact - it is for this very reason that I got into design.

01.

I believe in advocating for naked truth first and foremost. This means I investigate and collaborate with stakeholders, designers, users and any related parties I can get my hands on, to discover the insights that allow for simple, intuitive and impactful designs.

02.

I’m motivated to fail fast, cheap and early so the right solution is found before the fallacy of sunk costs takes over. Investing time up front leads to win-wins for organisations and the people they serve, and gives products and services the best opportunity to thrive in the darwinian free market.

03.

I see the world as a complex and interconnected set of systems. As such, I want to understand and learn from diverse industries and groups of people, believing every project will inform the next. I appreciate the multidisciplinary nature of UX Design, which aligns with my interests in psychology, futurism and philosophy.

Six months immersed in the world of UX Design has deepened my understanding and competencies, but also enlightened me to the relevant experience and soft-skills that translate from my professional and educational history:

Six months of 'Design in Visual Communication' at UTS taught me fundamentals in typography, markings & composition.

A Bachelor's degree in journalism at CSU taught me critical thinking, writing, debate, presenting, research and storytelling.

A news cadetship at 2MCE local radio taught me interviewing, design for audience, presenting & mentoring.

Online & offline self-education taught me emotional resonance, leadership and front-end development fundamentals (HTML, CSS & JS).

Video editing lead stories on national news bulletins taught me stakeholder engagement, visual storytelling and working under the pressure.
