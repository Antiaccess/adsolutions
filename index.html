Hey! I'm Anthony, a contemplative and conceptual thinker with an aptitude for research, collaboration & innovative, future-minded, interface design.

With curiosity in User Experience & Service Design fuelled by a recent visit to Texas' South by South-West conference (SXSW2017), I returned committed to solving big problems, designing scientifically and doing impactful work. Immediately I was attending conferences, meetup's and having coffees with UXer's who convinced me to step back from a full-time role as a non-linear editor at Channel Nine and spend two-and-a-half months immersed in full-time, practical UX study at General Assembly.

01.

I believe in advocating for naked truth first and foremost. This means I investigate and collaborate with stakeholders, designers, users and any related parties I can get my hands on, to discover the insights that allow for simple, intuitive and impactful designs.

02.

I’m motivated to fail fast, cheap and early so the right solution is found before the fallacy of sunk costs takes over. Investing time up front leads to win-wins for organisations and the people they serve, and gives products and services the best opportunity to thrive in the darwinian free market.

03.

I see the world as a complex and interconnected set of systems. As such, I want to understand and learn from diverse industries and groups of people, believing every project will inform the next. I appreciate the multidisciplinary nature of UX Design, which aligns with my interests in psychology, futurism and philosophy.

Although I'm new to the world of UX Design, I pull a lot of relevant experience and soft-skills from my professional and educational history:

  • from 6 months at UTS studying Visual Communication, I bring fundamentals in typography, markings, composition and adobe software.
  • from studying journalism at CSU, I bring critical thinking, writing, debate, presenting, interviewing, research synthesis and storytelling.
  • from presenting news bulletins on 2MCE local radio, I bring interviewing, rundown design and on-air presenting.
  • from my own personal study, I bring emotional intelligence and front-end development fundamentals (HTML, CSS & JS).
  • from news video editing at Channel 9, I bring stakeholder engagement, visual storytelling, leadership skills and working under the pressure of tight deadlines.

I am currently freelancing at Channel 9, whilst working as a UX/UI designer on a side-project in the automotive industry.
